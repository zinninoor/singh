The Hf cows

The Hf cows are considered to be the high production milk cattle. These cows gives 25 liters of milk per day and are also used to make panner, tofu, milkshake, milk drinks etc. high milk hf cow

Sahiwal Cows

Sahiwal Cows are indigenous cattle of the India and are from Punjab region. The cows have great red color, long height, heavy weight and give high production of milk.

Murrah Buffaloes

Murrah Buffaloes have black- white patches, 650kg weight and provide high production of milk. The milk consist of high amount of fats, protein, calcium and give pure nutrient enriched milk.

Bulls

The Bulls of milk have good amount of fat, butterfat, calcium and high quantity of protein. These are consider to be the best in giving high production of milk.
